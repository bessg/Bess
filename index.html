About Me✍(◔◡◔)

My real name is Elizabeth, but most people call me Bess. I'm a writer, artist, activist and love music, nature and coffee. I take film photography as well! Ask me about spoken word poetry, film photography, service based learning, or my current favorite bass rift.

I'm a Leo sun☀, Taurus moon☾, Leo rising⬆

☮My Childhood, Family, and Home☮

My whole life, I've been surrounded by music and nature. My family has helped me grow my relationship with both those things. I lived in New Jersey for most of my life, until I was 14 and moved away to PA. I was able to get a scholarship to a boarding school, where I lived most of my high school life.

Above are pictires of my beach town, my family, and my childhood on the farm. My family is still really important to me; I live with my mother, father, sister, and Cat. I live with them in the off-months in Ocean Grove, NJ, 4 blocks from the beach.

✍My Work✍

Above is the cover of my book, Let Me Grow, published in 2019. Along with poetry, I also create different types of videos on Youtube showcasing some of my poetry. I really enjoy creating multimedia digital projects, along with performing my pieces to audiences. I've also dabbled in creative non-fiction writing, having multiple poems and short stories published to literary magazine!

I've even made a podcast episode for a journalism class!

I'm currently completing a degree in Professional Writing, Media and Communications. Check back for more projects!
